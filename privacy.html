Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 5, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to Emulation Revival ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how your information is collected, used, and protected when you visit our website hosted on GitHub Pages.

By using our website, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

2. Information We Collect

Because our website is a static site designed to provide resources and community links, we collect very little personal data. However, certain information is collected automatically by our hosting provider or when you voluntarily submit it.

  • Automatically Collected Information (Log Data): Our website is hosted on GitHub Pages. When you visit the site, GitHub automatically collects standard server log information. This may include your IP address, browser type, operating system, referring URLs, device information, and the date/time of your visit. We do not have direct access to this raw personal data; it is managed by GitHub.
  • Voluntarily Submitted Information: If you contact us using our embedded Google Form, we will collect the information you choose to provide (such as your name, email address, and the contents of your message).

3. Cookies and Local Storage

We do not use tracking cookies or third-party advertising cookies.

We do use Local Storage strictly for functional purposes to improve your user experience:

  • Theme Preference: We use your browser's Local Storage to remember your preference for Light Mode or Dark Mode (saved as theme). This data never leaves your device and is never transmitted to us or any third parties.

4. How We Use Your Information

We use the limited information we collect for the following purposes:

  • To maintain and improve the site: To ensure the site renders properly on your device and remembers your viewing preferences.
  • To respond to inquiries: To read and reply to messages you submit via our contact form.
  • To monitor security: Relying on GitHub's infrastructure to protect the website against malicious activity.

5. Third-Party Links and Services

Our website serves as a hub and contains numerous links to third-party websites, including Discord, emulator download pages, and GitHub repositories.

Please Note: We are not responsible for the privacy practices, content, or data collection policies of any third-party websites. Once you click an external link (such as joining a Discord server or downloading a file from a third-party developer), their respective privacy policies apply.

Additionally, we utilize Google Forms for our contact page. Information submitted through this form is subject to Google's Privacy Policy.

6. Children's Privacy

Our website is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal identifiable information from children. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so we can remove it.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for other operational, legal, or regulatory reasons. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Effective Date" at the top.

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions or suggestions about our Privacy Policy, do not hesitate to contact us:

  • Via Discord: Join our Revives Community server and open a ticket.
  • Via Contact Form: Visit our Contact Page.

For further support, please join our Discord community.
